1. Who we are

Axcended is a South African learning and mentorship platform that connects high-school and university learners with tutors, mentors, classes, games, study groups and other educational content.

When we say “Axcended”, “we”, or “us” in this policy, we are referring to the team that designs, builds and operates the Axcended app and website.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you give to us

We collect information you provide directly when you use Axcended, including:

  • Account details – your name, email address, password, and basic profile information such as grade, school, university or study interests.
  • Tutor / mentor profile details – if you sign up as a tutor or mentor, we may collect extra information such as subjects you teach, short bio, qualifications and links you choose to share.
  • Content and activity – messages, posts, comments, homework uploads, session notes, answers in quizzes and games, and any files you choose to upload.
  • Subscription and payment information – the type of plan you choose, purchase history, renewal status and transaction IDs. Payments are processed by Google Play or other payment providers; we do not store your full card details.
  • Support requests – information you send us when you contact support (for example screenshots, crash descriptions or feedback).

2.2 Information we collect automatically (usage data)

When you use the app or website, we automatically collect some information about how it is used so we can keep Axcended secure and improve the experience. This may include:

  • Device information (device model, operating system, app version, language).
  • IP address and approximate location (e.g. country or region), mainly to keep the platform safe and prevent abuse.
  • Pages and screens you view, buttons you tap, time spent in different sections, and general usage patterns.
  • Crash logs and diagnostic information that help us fix bugs and improve performance.

2.3 Information from third-party sign-in

If you choose to sign in with Google, Apple, Facebook or other providers, we receive basic profile information from that service (such as your name, email address and a unique identifier), according to the permissions you grant there.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Let you create and manage your Axcended account and profile.
  • Match students with tutors, mentors, classes, groups and challenges.
  • Run learning features – including lessons, quizzes, games, progress tracking and other tools inside the app.
  • Process subscriptions and in-app purchases, and show you the correct access, plans and wallets inside the app.
  • Communicate with you about updates, class reminders, notifications, security alerts and important changes to Axcended.
  • Analyse usage so we can understand what is working, fix issues and build better features for students and tutors.
  • Protect the Axcended community by detecting spam, abuse, cheating and security risks.
  • Comply with our legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use.

If we introduce AI-powered features (for example automatic marking or study recommendations), we will clearly explain how those features use your content and results inside the app.

4. Cookies and tracking technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and understand how the site is used. A cookie is a small file stored on your device.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies — required to keep you logged in and secure and to run core features of the website.
  • Preference cookies — remember things like language or display settings.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how people discover and use Axcended so we can improve it.
  • Marketing cookies — may be used to promote Axcended on other platforms and measure campaign performance.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. If you block certain cookies, some parts of the Service may not work as expected.

5. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only when it is needed to run Axcended or when we are legally required to. This may include:

  • Service providers who host our servers, send emails and notifications, provide analytics and crash reporting, or process payments on our behalf.
  • Tutors, mentors and learners – your name and profile information may be visible to people you connect with in classes, groups or sessions.
  • App stores and payment providers – such as Google Play Billing, to manage purchases and subscriptions.
  • Legal and safety – when we believe it is necessary to comply with the law, respond to a lawful request, or protect the rights, property and safety of Axcended, our users or others.
  • Business changes – if we ever participate in a merger, acquisition or similar event, your information may be transferred as part of that process, but this Privacy Policy will continue to apply unless you are told otherwise.

6. Data retention & account deletion

We keep your information only for as long as it is needed for the purposes described in this policy, including any legal or reporting requirements.

If you decide that Axcended is no longer for you, you can request deletion of your account. When you do:

  • We delete or anonymise the personal data we no longer need.
  • We may keep limited records (for example transaction history needed for tax and accounting) for the period required by law.

You can request account deletion from within the app (where the feature is available) or by emailing us at axcended1@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account.

7. Your rights and choices

We want you to feel in control of your information. Depending on your location and local laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct information that is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Request deletion of your account and certain personal data.
  • Object to or request that we limit certain types of processing.
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on your consent (for example for marketing emails).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in the “Contact us” section. We may ask you to verify your identity before completing your request.

8. Security of your data

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, such as encryption in transit, secure hosting and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot promise absolute security.

If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately so we can help.

9. Children and students

Axcended is designed for students and young people, often with the involvement of parents, guardians, schools or tutors. We encourage responsible use of the app and open conversations between students, parents and educators about privacy and safety online.

If you believe we have collected personal information from a child in a way that is inappropriate or without the right permissions, please let us know and we will investigate and take action where needed.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as Axcended evolves. When we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. In some cases, we may also notify you in the app or by email.
