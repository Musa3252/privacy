Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected – Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Email address
  • First name and last name
  • Profile information such as grade, subjects, school or university
  • Payment-related information (processed via app stores / payment providers)
  • Any content you upload (messages, posts, homework, files)

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. Usage Data may include information such as your device’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, device type, operating system, app version, the pages or screens you visit, time and date of your visit, time spent on those pages, crash logs and other diagnostic data.

Information from Third-Party Services

If you sign in using Google, Apple, Facebook or other providers, we may receive basic profile information (such as your name, email address and an ID) from that service, depending on your settings with that provider.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our website and to store certain information. Cookies are small files placed on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. Some parts of the Service may not function properly if you disable cookies.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies — required for login, security and core features.
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings (for example language).
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how users interact with the Service.
  • Marketing cookies — may be used to show Axcended ads on other platforms.

How We Share Your Personal Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate, improve and support the Service.
  • Payment processors and app stores that handle subscriptions and purchases.
  • Tutors and learners where needed to run classes, chats or groups.
  • Law enforcement or regulators when required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of users.
  • Another organisation in connection with a merger, acquisition or similar event, where your information may be transferred as an asset.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your account and certain stored data.
  • Object to or restrict some types of processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details in the “Contact Us” section below.

Security of Your Personal Data

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the “Last updated” date at the top. In some cases, we may also notify you via the app or by email.
